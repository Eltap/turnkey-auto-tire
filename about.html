Family-Owned Since Day One

Turnkey Auto & Tire was started 11 years ago by a mechanic who wanted to run a shop the way he'd want to be treated as a customer — straight answers, fair prices, no pressure to fix things that don't need fixing.

Today, the same philosophy runs the shop: we tell you what's actually wrong with your car, what it'll cost, and let you make the call.
